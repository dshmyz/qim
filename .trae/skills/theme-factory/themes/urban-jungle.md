# Urban Jungle

A fresh and vibrant theme that blends urban sophistication with tropical greenery, perfect for modern eco-conscious brands.

## Color Palette

- **Concrete Gray**: `#2c3e50` - Primary background color
- **Jade Green**: `#27ae60` - Main accent color
- **Leafy Lime**: `#2ecc71` - Secondary accent
- **Terracotta**: `#d35400` - Warm contrast
- **Sand Beige**: `#ecf0f1` - Light backgrounds
- **White Cloud**: `#ffffff` - Text

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Sustainable business presentations, eco-friendly product launches, wellness brands, organic marketing materials, green tech conferences.
