# Spring Blossom

A fresh and delicate theme inspired by spring flowers, featuring soft pinks, lavenders, and fresh greens.

## Color Palette

- **Blush Pink**: `#ffcdd2` - Light background
- **Cherry Blossom**: `#f8bbd9` - Primary accent
- **Lavender**: `#e1bee7` - Secondary accent
- **Fresh Green**: `#c8e6c9` - Natural contrast
- **Soft Yellow**: `#fff9c4` - Highlighting
- **Deep Plum**: `#4a148c` - Text

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans Light

## Best Used For

Wedding invitations, spring event promotions, beauty brand launches, floral workshops, feminine product marketing, garden party materials.
