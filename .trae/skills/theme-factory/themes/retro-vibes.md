# Retro Vibes

A nostalgic and playful theme inspired by 80s and 90s aesthetics, featuring bold neon colors and geometric patterns.

## Color Palette

- **Midnight Black**: `#1e1e1e` - Primary background color
- **Neon Pink**: `#ff6b9d` - Main accent color
- **Electric Blue**: `#00d4ff` - Secondary accent
- **Sunset Orange**: `#ff9500` - Tertiary accent
- **Vintage Yellow**: `#ffeb3b` - Highlighting
- **Off White**: `#f0f0f0` - Text

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Creative agency pitches, retro-themed events, music festival promotions, nostalgic brand campaigns, pop culture presentations.
