# Mediterranean Dream

A warm and inviting theme inspired by the Mediterranean coast, featuring sandy beaches, terracotta rooftops, and azure waters.

## Color Palette

- **Terracotta**: `#c0392b` - Primary accent color
- **Azure Blue**: `#3498db` - Secondary accent
- **Sandy Beige**: `#f5deb3` - Light backgrounds
- **Olive Green**: `#808000` - Natural contrast
- **Deep Navy**: `#1a2332` - Dark elements
- **Bright White**: `#ffffff` - Text

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Travel and tourism presentations, hospitality industry materials, culinary events, lifestyle brands, cultural festivals, real estate promotions.
