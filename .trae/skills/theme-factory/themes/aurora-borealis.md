# Aurora Borealis

A mesmerizing and ethereal theme inspired by the Northern Lights, featuring glowing greens, purples, and blues that dance across the night sky.

## Color Palette

- **Deep Space**: `#0a0e27` - Primary background color
- **Aurora Green**: `#50c878` - Main accent color
- **Cosmic Purple**: `#9b59b6` - Secondary accent
- **Stardust Blue**: `#3498db` - Tertiary accent
- **Northern Light**: `#74b9ff` - Highlighting elements
- **Celestial White**: `#f5f6fa` - Text and light backgrounds

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans Light

## Best Used For

Technology conferences, scientific presentations, creative workshops, futuristic product launches, cosmic-themed events, inspirational talks.
