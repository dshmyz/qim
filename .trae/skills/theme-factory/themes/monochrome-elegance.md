# Monochrome Elegance

A sophisticated and timeless theme using only black, white, and gray tones, perfect for high-end luxury brands.

## Color Palette

- **Pure Black**: `#000000` - Primary background
- **Charcoal**: `#333333` - Secondary dark
- **Slate Gray**: `#666666` - Medium tone
- **Silver**: `#999999` - Light gray
- **Pearl White**: `#f5f5f5` - Light background
- **Bright White**: `#ffffff` - Text

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Serif

## Best Used For

Luxury brand presentations, high-end fashion shows, premium product launches, art gallery exhibitions, fine dining promotions, exclusive event invitations.
